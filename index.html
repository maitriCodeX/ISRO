Gaganyaan Mission: India's First Crewed Spaceflight


Gaganyaan is ISRO's ambitious mission to send Indian astronauts (Gagannauts) into space for the first time. The mission aims to place a crew of 2-3 astronauts in low Earth orbit (~400 km) for up to 3 days and bring them back safely.
